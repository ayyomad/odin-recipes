Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It is baked until

golden and bubbly, creating a comforting and hearty meal. Variations include vegetarian, seafood, and even dessert versions

.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion & garlic
  • Mozzarella cheese (for melting)
  • Eggs

steps

  1. Prepare the Sauce – Cook ground meat (or veggies) with onion, garlic, tomato sauce, and seasonings. Simmer.
  2. Mix the Cheese Filling – Combine ricotta, eggs, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  3. Layer the Lasagna – In a baking dish, layer sauce, pasta sheets, ricotta mix, and mozzarella. Repeat.
  4. Bake – Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes. Remove foil, bake until golden.
  5. Rest & Serve – Let it sit for 10 minutes before slicing. Enjoy!
