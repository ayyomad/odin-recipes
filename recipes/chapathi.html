chapathi

Description

Chapati is a soft, whole wheat Indian flatbread that is a staple in many households. It is made without yeast, cooked on a hot pan, and can be enjoyed with curries, vegetables, or pickles.

ight, healthy, and easy to make, chapati is a versatile and nutritious dish.

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat flour (atta)
  • salt
  • water
  • Oil or ghee (optional)
  • FFlour (for dusting)

steps

  1. Prepare the Dough – Mix whole wheat flour, salt, and water; knead into a soft dough.
  2. Rest the Dough – Cover and let it rest for 20–30 minutes.
  3. Roll Out – Divide into small balls, dust with flour, and roll into thin circles.
  4. Cook on a Pan – Heat a pan, cook chapati until bubbles form, flip, and press for even cooking.
  5. SServe – Optionally brush with ghee and serve warm with curry or dal.
