Dosa

Description

Dosa is a thin, crispy South Indian pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It is a popular breakfast dish, often served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Light and nutritious, dosa comes in various styles, including masala dosa stuffed with spiced potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • salt
  • water
  • onion
  • Fenugreek seeds

steps

  1. Soak & Grind – Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 6 hours, then grind into a smooth batter.
  2. Ferment – Let the batter ferment overnight until it rises and turns slightly tangy.
  3. Prepare the Batter – Add salt and water to adjust consistency.
  4. Cook the Dosa – Spread a thin layer of batter on a hot, greased pan and cook until crispy.
  5. Serve – Fold and serve hot with chutney and sambar. Enjoy!
